Enable OAuth Providers in Firebase Console
Open your project at https://console.firebase.google.com

Select Authentication → Sign‑in method.

Enable Google provider:

Click Google, toggle Enable, set a support email, Save.

Enable Facebook provider:

Click Facebook, toggle Enable.

You must supply your Facebook App ID & App Secret (from your Facebook Developer dashboard).

Ensure the OAuth redirect URI shown in Firebase is added in your FB app’s Valid OAuth Redirect URIs.

Save.

(Android only) Under Project settings → Your apps → SHA certificate fingerprints, add your debug & release SHA‑1.
